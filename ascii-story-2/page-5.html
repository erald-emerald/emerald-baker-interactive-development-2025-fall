INSIDE THE HOUSE

The wallpaper peels in long strips, revealing not wood or plaster, but… teeth. Human teeth. Lots of them. The chandelier groans as if it’s alive.

Suddenly, a butler appears out of nowhere, dressed in black and smiling far too wide. “Dinner is served,” he says, placing a plate with exactly one chicken nugget before you.

The walls… are alive.
